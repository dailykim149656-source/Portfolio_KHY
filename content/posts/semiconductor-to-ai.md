# From Semiconductor Process Thinking to AI Systems

Semiconductor process work rewards controlled change, measurement discipline, and root-cause analysis. Those habits transfer well into AI systems work.

## Transferable habits

1. Define the measurable failure mode before changing the process.
2. Keep experiments small enough to explain.
3. Separate correlation from a deployable operating rule.

In AI applications, the same mindset shows up as evaluation sets, smoke checks, observability, and rollback plans.
